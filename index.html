About Chaos

IIMA Chaos is the Biggest B-School Cultural Festival in Asia and Biggest College Event in Gujarat attracting a footfall of over 60,000 people over 4 days including 12,000 participants from over 600 national and 50 international institutes & colleges.

IIMA Chaos is a 4-day event to be held from 23rd January to 26th January 2016. It will feature performances by professional artists on all the 4 evenings with cultural competitions in dance, music and fashion, workshops, gaming, dramatics, literary, quizzing, painting and a lot of fun events at all times during the day. The performances and events are structured to excite and engage the youth.
